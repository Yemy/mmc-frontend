Together We Restore Hope & Build Resilience

MMC is guided by human dignity, gender equality, and social justice. We adopt a survivor-centered, rights-based approach to address sexual and gender-based violence, supporting vulnerable women, girls, and internally displaced communities in Tigray.

Empowering Survivors

Providing survivor-centered psychosocial support and skills training for women and girls affected by sGBV.

Supporting Communities

Delivering humanitarian aid and livelihood support to internally displaced and conflict-affected communities.

  • Supported 727 displaced students with educational materials in Mekelle
  • Delivered livelihood support to 72 war-affected women in Agulae, Tigray
  • Assisted 668 individuals in Tselemti and Kolla Tembien Woredas affected by conflict
